Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2025  ·  Last updated: April 19, 2025

✅ Our apps collect no personal data

Who we are

Pecan Valley Studio is an independent app development studio by Earl Claus. We build practical tools for everyday makers and hobbyists.

What data we collect

None. Pecan Valley Studio apps do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. We have no servers, no user accounts, and no analytics.

What stays on your device

Some apps allow you to save data (such as recipes). That data is stored only on your device using Apple's local storage. It never leaves your phone. Deleting the app removes all locally saved data.

Third-party services

Our apps do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Children's privacy

We do not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. Our apps are intended for adult hobbyists and makers.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this page and the effective date above. We will never collect personal data without clearly disclosing it here first.
