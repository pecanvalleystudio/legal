Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2025  ·  Last updated: April 19, 2025

✅ This app collects no personal data

What data we collect

None. EasySoap does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

What stays on your device

When you save a recipe, it is stored only on your device using Apple's local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your phone. Deleting the app removes all saved recipes.

Third-party services

EasySoap does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

Children's privacy

EasySoap does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is intended for adults engaged in soap-making as a hobby or profession.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change our data practices, we will update this page and the effective date above. We will never collect personal data without clearly disclosing it here first.
