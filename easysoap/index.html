Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2025  ·  Last updated: April 19, 2025

✅ This app collects no personal data

Who we are

EasySoap is a soap-making calculator app developed by Earl Claus at Pecan Valley Studio. It helps hobbyists and professional soapmakers calculate oil weights, lye amounts, and recipe ratios.

What data we collect

None. EasySoap does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no user accounts, no servers, and no analytics of any kind.

What stays on your device

When you save a soap recipe, it is stored only on your device using Apple's local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your phone and is never accessible to us. Deleting the app permanently removes all saved recipes.

Third-party services

EasySoap does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporters, or tracking services.

Children's privacy

EasySoap does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is intended for adults engaged in soap-making as a hobby or profession. It involves instructions for handling caustic chemicals and is not appropriate for unsupervised use by children.

Changes to this policy

If our data practices ever change, we will update this page and the effective date above before the change takes effect. We will never collect personal data without clearly disclosing it here first.
